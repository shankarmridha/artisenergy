Project

Within

← All projects

22 KWh generated

Within serves as both a community centre and an observatory at night, producing natural harmonic tunes that stimulate the auditory senses.

By day it functions as a gathering place for the surrounding community; after dark, it becomes a place to look up and take in the sky, its structure tuned to turn ambient air movement into sound as it generates power.
