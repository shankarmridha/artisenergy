Project

Lucid

← All projects

65 KWh generated

A sculpture with a rhythmic flow of the water fountain, engaging and conversing with onlookers while illuminating at night.

Beyond the visual and acoustic experience, Lucid aerates the water it moves through, improving water quality and ecological balance while creating a tranquil effect for anyone nearby.
