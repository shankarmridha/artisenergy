About us

Art that carries its own weight

We engage in the creative process of conceptualizing, designing, and installing art. At the same time, we create community spaces that have utility.

Every Art is Energy installation starts as a public artwork first — a landmark meant to be looked at, gathered around, and remembered. We then engineer it to generate renewable energy from the forces already around it: sun, wind, water, and motion. The result is a structure that pays for its own upkeep while giving a place its identity.

Our multidisciplinary team of architects, artists, and energy engineers works with cities, campuses, and private sponsors to design installations suited to their site — from beachfront canopies to community observatories.
